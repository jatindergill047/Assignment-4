About Hot and Cool

HOT&COOLCLOTHING.CA provides you with the newest collection of styles from ONLY, VERO MODA, Noisy may, JACK & JONES, ONLY & SONS, and SELECTED HOMME/FEMME. We combine the latest fashion with editorial inspirational content.

HOT & COOL is a family-owned clothing and accessories company founded in CANADA in 1999 by Jatinder Singh Gill.

HOT & COOL provides fast affordable fashion for women, men, teenagers and children via a portfolio of over 20 different brands. HOT & COOL brands are marketed in 70 markets across most of Europe, the Middle East, Canada, India and globally via e-commerce. We have more than 3,000 branded chain stores across 38 markets worldwide and our products are sold in approx. 15,000 multi-brand and department stores. Our products are available online, in branded chain stores, in multi-brand and department stores.
